## Introduction
- Jeannine Hill Fletcher insists we cannot pursue justice without first addressing racial justice and equality.
- She details how white Christian power worked to strip Indigenous peoples of land through indoctrination, religious conversion, marriage, and white generational inheritance.
- The conversation explores Christian patriarchy, Indigenous matriarchy, and the importance of breaking matrilineal bonds to seize land.
