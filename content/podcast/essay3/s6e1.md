## Special Episode
This live recording of *The Doctrine of Christian Discovery, The Jesuits, and Laudato si: Defending Mother Earth* (June 14, 2025) coincided with the #NoKings protests. Speakers connect the Doctrine of Discovery, Jesuit roles in colonization, and the need for land return, language revitalization, and food sovereignty to heal people and planet.

### Outline
- Haudenosaunee Thanksgiving Address frames gratitude and responsibility to creation
- Seven generations ethic and the Great Tree of Peace at Onondaga Lake
- Environmental justice as Indigenous sovereignty; return land to restore ecosystems
- Colonial institutions displaced Indigenous knowledge; climate change is the compound effect
- Academic and Jesuit institutions must confront their colonial histories and pursue repair
